# Frequently Asked Questions

## CountLabs

### Is CountLabs a real-money gambling app?
No. CountLabs is for **education and practice only**.  
No real money is used, wagered, or exchanged.

### Does CountLabs collect personal data?
CountLabs does **not collect personal information**.  
Gameplay and training data remain on your device.

### I found a bug or issue
Please email us with the following details if possible:

- Device model (iPhone / iPad)
- iOS version
- What you were doing when the issue occurred

### Feature requests
We welcome feedback and feature suggestions.  
Send us an email and let us know how CountLabs can be improved.

## BudgetFlow

### What is BudgetFlow?
BudgetFlow is a budgeting and expense tracking application designed to help users monitor spending and manage personal finances.

### Does BudgetFlow collect personal financial data?
No. BudgetFlow stores budgeting and financial information **locally on your device**.  
This information is **not transmitted to external servers**.

### I found a bug or issue
Please email us with the following details if possible:

- Device model
- iOS version
- A description of the issue

### Feature requests
We welcome suggestions for improving BudgetFlow.  
Please send us an email with your ideas.
